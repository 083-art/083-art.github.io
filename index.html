About Me

I am Mrs. Faith Udosen — a trained biochemist, passionate nursing professional, and creative photographer. Though I studied biochemistry, my heart has always been in caregiving. I actively engage in nursing duties, driven by a desire to uplift women and families through health education and compassionate service. My photography reflects the same care I give to people — capturing stories with purpose and light.

My Mission

I envision an Africa — and a world — where women are empowered with knowledge about their bodies, their health, and their voices. I believe science and compassion are not separate; they are partners in change. Whether through care, education, or photography, I aim to make that change real.
